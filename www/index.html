FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
Do I need a Star Citizen account to use CrewFind? +
You don't need to verify ownership of a Star Citizen account, but you will need to provide your RSI handle (your in-game name from robertsspaceindustries.com) when setting up your profile. This is how other players identify you in the verse.
How do I log in? +
You can log in using your Discord account or register with an email address and password — both are free. Either method gives you a full CrewFind account, including a pilot profile, avatar upload, ship hangar, and all app features. Discord login is convenient if you already use Discord for Star Citizen, but there's no difference in what you can do once you're in.
How do I post a crew listing? +
Once logged in, hit the Post Listing button. Fill in your ship (searchable dropdown), mission type, number of crew slots, star system, and optionally your Discord username for people to contact you. Your listing goes live immediately and stays up for 48 hours before auto-expiring.
How do I apply to someone's listing? +
Click the Apply button on any listing. You'll see the ship commander's Discord handle (if they provided one) so you can reach out directly. Coordination happens in Discord — CrewFind is the matchmaking layer, not a chat platform.
How long do listings stay active? +
Listings auto-expire after a set period to keep the board fresh and free of stale posts. If your crew is still needed after a listing expires, just re-post — it only takes a few seconds.
Can I use CrewFind on mobile? +
Yes — the web version at sc-crewfind.com is fully mobile-responsive and works great on any phone or tablet browser. A dedicated Android app is currently in development and coming soon.
Is CrewFind affiliated with Cloud Imperium Games? +
No. CrewFind is an independent, community-built tool for Star Citizen players. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Cloud Imperium Games. Star Citizen® is a trademark of Cloud Imperium Games Corp.
Is CrewFind free? +
Completely free, no ads, no paywalls. CrewFind is a passion project built for the SC community.
I found a bug or have a suggestion — how do I report it? +
Jump into the CrewFind Discord server and drop your feedback in the appropriate channel. Bug reports and feature suggestions are always welcome.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

What We Collect

When you use CrewFind, we may collect:

  • Your Discord username and ID (if you log in via Discord)
  • Your email address (if you register with email)
  • Your RSI handle (provided by you in your profile)
  • Listing data you post (ship, mission type, star system, crew slots)

How We Use It

Your information is used solely to operate CrewFind — displaying your listings, letting other players contact you via Discord, and keeping your account active. We do not sell, share, or monetize your data.

Discord OAuth

If you log in with Discord, we receive your Discord username and user ID from Discord's OAuth2 service. We do not receive your Discord password, email (unless you grant permission), or any private messages.

Data Storage

Data is stored securely via Supabase (PostgreSQL), hosted on AWS infrastructure. Listings auto-delete after 48 hours. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us via the CrewFind Discord.

Cookies & Analytics

CrewFind uses Supabase session cookies for authentication only. We do not use third-party analytics or advertising trackers.

Contact

For any privacy concerns, reach out via the CrewFind Discord server.
